Mental Health and Wellness

Introduction to Mental Health:-

Mental health and wellness are fundamental aspects of our overall well-being. They encompass a broad range of factors that influence how we think, feel, and behave on a daily basis. Unlike physical health, mental health is often intangible and can be harder to quantify, but its importance is just as significant. Mental health refers to our emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It affects how we handle stress, relate to others, and make choices in our lives.

Additionally, reducing stigma surrounding mental health issues and achieving a balance between work, personal life, and leisure activities are vital components of mental wellness. By prioritizing mental health and incorporating these practices into our daily lives, we can enhance resilience, cope with stress more effectively, and lead fulfilling and meaningful lives.
